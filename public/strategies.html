üìà Moving Average Crossover Beginner

How It Works:

This strategy uses two moving averages (fast and slow) to identify trend changes. When the fast MA crosses above the slow MA, it signals a potential uptrend (buy signal). When the fast MA crosses below the slow MA, it signals a potential downtrend (sell signal).
